Clouds

2020

Why is it your favorite movie?
The story behind the movie was moving in its attempt to relay the idea of not waiting for tomorrow to live for today. In other words we must enjoy the present and make the most of every single day so that we may come to appreciate our experiences.
A brief description of the movie?
Zach Sobiech suffers from osteosarcoma and is notified he has months to live. The high school senior takes no time to experience all that he can before his final hour.
