A brief description of the movie?
Nibbled by a radioactive insect in the tram, Brooklyn youngster Miles Morales abruptly creates baffling forces that change him into the unrivaled Spider-Man. At the point when he meets Peter Parker, he before long understands that there are numerous other people who share his extraordinary, high-flying abilities. Miles should now utilize his newly discovered abilities to fight the detestable Kingpin, a bulky maniac who can open entrances to different universes and maneuver various forms of Spider-Man into our reality.
