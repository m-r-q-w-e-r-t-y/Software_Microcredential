The Flower We Saw That Day

2013

Why is it your favorite movie?
The Flower We Saw That Day gives an interesting take on emotional maturity among teenagers in their quest to for fulfill the needs of a longtime friend who died in their youth. The act of coming together both emotionally and personally after years of growing apart is beautiful.
A brief description of the movie?
Jintan, a troubled teen, reminisces the day his friend, Menma, died in his youth. She comes to him as a ghost tasking him with the goal of redressing a fault in his life. They are unsure of what to exactly redress and so they carry on together to reach their goal.
