Hachi: A Dog's Tale

2009

Why is it your favorite movie?
The movie captures the carrying nature of animals and the emotional connection they hold to their human partners. It made me realize that they to have their own complex thinking that are similar to our own. They care for us unconditionally.
A brief description of the movie?
An American college professor stumbles upon a crate from Japan which he finds out was delivered from Japan in which it hold a Japanese pup breed. He names the dog Hachi and for years he care for the dog talking him everyday to the train station where he takes the train to commute to work. One evening the professor dies of a heart attack and Hachi remains waiting for his partner in the freezing cold, until he dies awaiting him.
